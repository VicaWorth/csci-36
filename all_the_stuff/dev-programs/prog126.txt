                        <h3 id="at-a-glance">Program Requirements</h3>

                        <div class="faqs pb-15">
                            <div class="faq">
                                <div class="question" role="tablist" id="faqHeading">
                                    <h5>
                                        <a href="#faqOne" aria-controls="faqOne" data-toggle="collapse" aria-expanded="true" class="extra-bold">
                                            Associate in Arts Degree in Language Arts <i class="fa fa-plus adjusted"></i>
                                        </a>
                                    </h5>
                                </div>
                                <div id="faqOne" class="collapse answer in pt-15" aria-labelledby="faqHeading" role="tabpanel" aria-expanded="true">
                                    <p><strong>Program Code:</strong> 11131.00AA</p>
                                    <p><strong>Type:</strong> Local</p>
                                    <p><strong>General Education:</strong> Butte Local</p>


                                    <p class="pt-15"><strong>Student Learning Outcomes:</strong></p>
                                    <p>Upon successful completion of this program, the student will be able to:</p>
                                    <ul class="dots">
                                            <li><p>Assess the applications of a Language Arts AA Degree for personal advancement in educational and/or professional goals.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Identify, employ and evaluate different frameworks and perspectives for analyzing and interpreting literature or communication artifacts. These frameworks may include political, socioeconomic, geographical, ethnic, cultural, ecological, psychoanalytical, historical, gender, sexuality, and genre development.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Demonstrate oral, aural and non-verbal competence as appropriate to the discipline, or language.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Define ethical communication and explain the principles that it encompasses, regardless the discipline, language or medium.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Research, organize and create written, oral, and non-verbal messages using appropriate resources.</p></li>
                                    </ul>
                                    
                                        <p class="pt-15"><a class="program-map btn btn-yellow" id="mapLink" href="/ProgramMap/12/2475">Click Here for Program Map</a></p>

                                    <div class="row pt-20 pb-5 mt-5">

                                            <strong>
18 - 19                                                Units
                                            </strong>
                                        </div>
                                    </div>


                                            <div class="row pt-10">
