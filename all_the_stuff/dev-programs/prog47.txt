                    <div class="col-md-12">
                        <h3>About the Program</h3>
                        <p><p>Students completing Associate Degrees for Transfer are guaranteed admission to the CSU system. Please see the beginning of the &ldquo;Academic Programs&rdquo; section for details.<br /><br />This program teaches students to embrace a practitioner-scholar model that inspires and supports the development of knowledge, skills and dispositions essential to fostering healthy growth and learning of children and families in a diverse society, including leadership and advocacy, for children up to age eighteen.</p></p>

                        <hr />
                    </div>
                </div>
                <div class="row" id="degrees">
                    <div class="col-md-12">
                        <h3 id="at-a-glance">Program Requirements</h3>

                        <div class="faqs pb-15">
                            <div class="faq">
                                <div class="question" role="tablist" id="faqHeading">
                                    <h5>
                                        <a href="#faqOne" aria-controls="faqOne" data-toggle="collapse" aria-expanded="true" class="extra-bold">
                                            Associate in Arts for Transfer Degree in Child and Adolescent Development <i class="fa fa-plus adjusted"></i>
                                        </a>
                                    </h5>
                                </div>
                                <div id="faqOne" class="collapse answer in pt-15" aria-labelledby="faqHeading" role="tabpanel" aria-expanded="true">
                                    <p><strong>Program Code:</strong> 35197.01AA-T</p>
                                    <p><strong>Type:</strong> Transfer</p>
                                    <p><strong>General Education:</strong> CSU, IGETC</p>


                                    <p class="pt-15"><strong>Student Learning Outcomes:</strong></p>
                                    <p>Upon successful completion of this program, the student will be able to:</p>
                                    <ul class="dots">
                                            <li><p>Synthesize research-based theories including principles and practices of child and adolescent development and learning.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Utilize critical thinking skills to analyze, evaluate, and make decisions concerning complex contemporary issues and the interactions among individuals and across societies.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Utilize scientific methodologies to research human development from infancy through adolescence.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Exhibit the ability to use evidence based assessment systems and curriculum planning systems to support children&#x27;s learning and development.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Knowledge of development in all learning domains from conception through adolescent, including knowledge about typical and atypical development.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Demonstrates the ability to promote developmentally, culturally and linguistically appropriate strategies to address diverse approaches to learning.</p></li>
                                    </ul>
                                    
                                        <p class="pt-15"><a class="program-map btn btn-yellow" id="mapLink" href="/ProgramMap/12/2584">Click Here for Program Map</a></p>
