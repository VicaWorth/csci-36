                                <div class="question" role="tablist" id="faqHeading">
                                    <h5>
                                        <a href="#faqOne" aria-controls="faqOne" data-toggle="collapse" aria-expanded="true" class="extra-bold">
                                            Associate in Arts Degree in Kinesiology <i class="fa fa-plus adjusted"></i>
                                        </a>
                                    </h5>
                                </div>
                                <div id="faqOne" class="collapse answer in pt-15" aria-labelledby="faqHeading" role="tabpanel" aria-expanded="true">
                                    <p><strong>Program Code:</strong> 01320.02AA</p>
                                    <p><strong>Type:</strong> Local</p>
                                    <p><strong>General Education:</strong> Butte Local</p>


                                    <p class="pt-15"><strong>Student Learning Outcomes:</strong></p>
                                    <p>Upon successful completion of this program, the student will be able to:</p>
                                    <ul class="dots">
                                            <li><p>Identify and describe major concepts, theoretical principles, history and evolution, and current trends in kinesiology.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Assess his or her own physical activity level as it relates to lifelong fitness.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Describe physical activity as it relates to physiological responses and adaptations to exercise.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Recognize anatomical impacts on exercise related to age, gender, individual goals, and special needs.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Demonstrate and apply scientific principles to movement in the specific areas of fitness, aquatics, combatives, individual sport and team sport.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Develop and modify proper safety techniques for a variety of fitness programs.</p></li>
                                    </ul>
