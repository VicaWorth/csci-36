                    <div class="col-md-12">
                        <h3>About the Program</h3>
                        <p><p>This is the second tier of the Medical Office Administration (MOA) career pathway. Students completing this next level of certificate will take courses that are designed to build on their MOA solid foundation and allow students an opportunity to become proficient in the in-demand knowledge and skills sought out by the healthcare industry.</p>

<p>Career related fields for the MOA include Medical Office Receptionist, Medical Office Assistant, Front Office Representative, Medical Records Administrative Assistant, Insurance Verification Specialist, and Medical Intake Specialist. This certificate of achievement can be completed in one year.</p></p>

                        <hr />
                    </div>
                </div>

                        <div class="faqs pb-15">
                            <div class="faq">
                                <div class="question" role="tablist" id="faqHeading">
                                    <h5>
                                        <a href="#faqOne" aria-controls="faqOne" data-toggle="collapse" aria-expanded="true" class="extra-bold">
                                            Certificate of Achievement in Medical Office Administration <i class="fa fa-plus adjusted"></i>
                                        </a>
                                    </h5>
                                </div>
                                <div id="faqOne" class="collapse answer in pt-15" aria-labelledby="faqHeading" role="tabpanel" aria-expanded="true">
                                    <p><strong>Program Code:</strong> 01315.01CA</p>
                                    <p><strong>Type:</strong> Career</p>
                                    <p><strong>General Education:</strong> None</p>


                                    <p class="pt-15"><strong>Student Learning Outcomes:</strong></p>
                                    <p>Upon successful completion of this program, the student will be able to:</p>
                                    <ul class="dots">
                                            <li><p>Apply knowledge of medical terminology to perform outpatient/physician diagnostic coding, and determine outpatient/procedural coding based on the abstraction of medical records.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Analyze and interpret medical information and apply sound decision-making skills pertaining to the appropriate ICD and CPT codes needed to maximize compensation for services rendered.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Apply effective listening, verbal, written, interpersonal, and technological communication skills which prepare them for the outpatient medical workplace.</p></li>
                                    </ul>
