                    <div class="col-md-12">
                        <h3>About the Program</h3>
                        <p><p>The Agriculture Science program offers an AS degree for those students interested in a more general course of study. Students with a background in production agriculture, 4-H or FFA will find success in this program as they pursue a career with an emphasis in agriculture sciences, agriculture education, and agribusiness occupations. California ranks first in the nation in annual gross agricultural returns. The livestock industry and over 250 specialty crops provide a variety of career choices. Trained personnel are employed on ranches and farms, as well as the vast agriculture service industry. Career opportunities also exist with governmental agencies. Every year there are several openings in Agriculture Education in California as well as other states.</p></p>

                        <hr />
                    </div>
                </div>
                <div class="row" id="degrees">
                    <div class="col-md-12">
                        <h3 id="at-a-glance">Program Requirements</h3>

                        <div class="faqs pb-15">
                            <div class="faq">
                                <div class="question" role="tablist" id="faqHeading">
                                    <h5>
                                        <a href="#faqOne" aria-controls="faqOne" data-toggle="collapse" aria-expanded="true" class="extra-bold">
                                            Associate in Science Degree in Agriculture Science <i class="fa fa-plus adjusted"></i>
                                        </a>
                                    </h5>
                                </div>
                                <div id="faqOne" class="collapse answer in pt-15" aria-labelledby="faqHeading" role="tabpanel" aria-expanded="true">
                                    <p><strong>Program Code:</strong> 01298.02AS</p>
                                    <p><strong>Type:</strong> Career</p>
                                    <p><strong>General Education:</strong> Butte Local</p>


                                    <p class="pt-15"><strong>Student Learning Outcomes:</strong></p>
                                    <p>Upon successful completion of this program, the student will be able to:</p>
                                    <ul class="dots">
                                            <li><p>Identify and apply the principles and techniques of modern crop and livestock management.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Describe fundamental care practices for animals, including selection, handling and management.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Distinguish common types of agricultural pests including weeds, diseases, and insects.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Calibrate plant protection equipment including hand-held, ATV, trailer, and orchard sprayers.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Select appropriate fertilizers, basic application methods, and recognize their effect on plants and the environment.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Describe how breeding, genetics, and biotechnology are used to increase agricultural production.</p></li>
                                            <li><p>Demonstrate scientific evaluation skills including interpreting graphs and data from scientific papers.</p></li>
                                    </ul>
                                    
                                        <p class="pt-15"><a class="program-map btn btn-yellow" id="mapLink" href="/ProgramMap/12/2628">Click Here for Program Map</a></p>

                                    <div class="row pt-20 pb-5 mt-5">
                                        <div class="col-md-10"><strong>Required courses:</strong></div>
                                        <div class="col-md-2">
